Battle Rules

  • Multiple merges in one move increase attack power.
  • Attack power becomes black garbage blocks on the opponent board.
  • Garbage cannot merge and fades out as its timer runs down.
  • CPU is a practice opponent. Friend mode alternates turns on one device.

Friend Battle

This is the local battle foundation. Online friend matches can reuse the P1/P2 board state and synchronize it over WebSocket later.
